Hi, I’m Mel.

I’m a Pilates and strength coach, and I’ve spent several years supporting women to move better, build strength, and feel more capable in their bodies. My classes are designed to feel supportive, not overwhelming — with a focus on real strength, mobility, nervous system support, and long-term health."

Exercise shouldn't feel like a battle.

If you've ever felt pushed too hard, unsupported, or like you were constantly "falling behind," you aren't alone. These classes are different.

No drill-sergeants. No pressure to prove yourself. Just guidance and a structured way to rebuild strength at your own pace.

This is for you if...

Your body feels stiff, tight, or not like it used to.
You want to get stronger but feel unsure where to start.
Group classes have felt overwhelming or too fast.
You want to feel strong, capable, and like yourself again.
